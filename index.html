About the Temple

ఆలయం గురించి

A Sacred Sanctuary in Krishna Sila

కంచి శ్రీ శ్రీ శ్రీ విజయేంద్ర సరస్వతి స్వామి వారిచే నామకరణం చేయబడిన శ్రీ మహా త్రిపుర సుందరీ సమేత చంద్రమౌళీశ్వరస్వామి వారి అనుజ్ఞ గణపతి దేవస్థానము.

The Sri Maha Tripura Sundari Sametha Chandramouleeswara Swamy Vari Anujna Ganapathi Devasthanam is a sacred temple consecrated in the holy village of Perecharla, Medikonduru Mandal, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh.

The temple has been graciously named by His Holiness Sri Sri Sri Vijayendra Saraswati Swami of Kanchi Kamakoti Peetham, and is dedicated to Lord Chandramouleeswara seated alongside Goddess Maha Tripura Sundari, with Anujna Ganapathi as their divine attendant.

This Devasthanam is a marvel of devotion — built entirely in sacred Krishna Sila (black granite), following the ancient Shaiva Agama traditions in every stone, sculpture, and śilpa. ఈ దేవస్థానము ఆసాంతం కృష్ణశిల చే నిర్మించబడుట విశేషము.

The Divine Trinity

ఆరాధ్య దైవములు

Sri Chandramouleeswara Swamy

శ్రీ చంద్రమౌళీశ్వరస్వామి

The Supreme Lord Shiva who wears the crescent moon (Chandra) upon his matted locks. Worshipped as the eternal source of cosmic peace, knowledge, and liberation.

Sri Maha Tripura Sundari

శ్రీ మహా త్రిపుర సుందరీ దేవి

The Divine Mother — Adi Parashakti, embodiment of supreme beauty, compassion, and cosmic energy. The benevolent consort of Lord Chandramouleeswara, worshipped through the sacred Sri Vidya tradition.

Sri Anujna Ganapathi

శ్రీ అనుజ్ఞ గణపతి

The granter of divine permission and remover of obstacles. As the younger brother of Lord Subrahmanya and son of Shiva-Parvati, he presides as the foremost deity whose blessings precede all auspicious endeavours.
